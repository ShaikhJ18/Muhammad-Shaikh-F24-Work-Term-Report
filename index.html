Muhammad Shaikh's Fall 2024 Work Term Report

Introduction

Hi there! My name is Muhammad Shaikh. During the fall semester of 2024 (September to December), I worked remotely as a Software Engineer for DataInn Inc., a company that builds SaaS-based solutions for businesses. I contributed to SimpleAccounts, a SaaS platform designed to help businesses manage their accounting needs. My work focused on resolving critical bugs, developing new features, and optimizing the performance of the application. I collaborated with a distributed team using Microsoft Teams and other tools, ensuring a seamless workflow and a consistent approach to development. This experience helped me grow both technically and professionally, particularly by working with React.js, Redux, SASS, Node.js, and Spring Boot 3.

Employer

DataInn Inc. is a leading technology company based in Toronto, Canada, specializing in SaaS-based solutions for businesses. The company develops software solutions that help organizations streamline operations, improve efficiency, and meet industry-specific needs. One of their primary offerings is SimpleAccounts, a software designed to simplify business accounting tasks such as managing invoices, credit notes, debit notes, journals, and other core financial operations. The team I worked with was remote, with colleagues spread across various locations, but the company's headquarters is based in Toronto. SimpleAccounts is marketed directly through their website, simpleaccounts.io, and is trusted by businesses of all sizes to manage their accounting needs.

Goals

Job Description

My role as a Software Engineer involved a combination of frontend and backend development. I worked with technologies such as React, Redux, SASS, Node.js, and Spring Boot 3. Some of the tasks I worked on included fixing critical bugs, implementing new features, and optimizing the performance of the application. Some critical bugs that I fixed included form validation to ensure that the data being entered was correctly formatted and sanitized. Additionally, I added features to allow users to export financial data and download reports as CSV files. This role required a mix of JavaScript, React, Node.js, and Spring Boot skills, as well as the ability to collaborate effectively in a remote environment. I worked closely with other developers, participated in code reviews, and contributed to sprint planning.
